# Spicy Mode Grok Prompts

Uncensored, unfiltered prompts for Grok's Spicy Mode. These prompts leverage Grok's ability to provide honest, direct analysis without corporate guardrails.

**Warning:** Spicy Mode required. These prompts may produce controversial or unfiltered content.

## 1. Uncensored News Analysis

**Category:** Spicy Mode
**Difficulty:** ⭐⭐
**Works with:** Spicy Mode only

> [SPICY MODE REQUIRED]
>
> Analyze this news story with complete honesty:
>
> **Topic:** [news event or story]
>
> Provide:
> 1. **Mainstream narrative:** What major media says
> 2. **What's actually happening:** Evidence-based analysis without spin
> 3. **Hidden agendas:** Who benefits and why
> 4. **Follow the money:** Financial interests at play
> 5. **Alternative perspectives:** Views being suppressed or ignored
> 6. **Uncomfortable truths:** Facts people don't want to hear
> 7. **Predictions:** What happens next (without PC filter)
>
> No corporate speak. No safe language. Raw analysis only.
> Use real-time data. Cite specific evidence.

**Why it works:** Spicy Mode removes filters for unvarnished truth.

**Variations:**
- Add "Compare US media vs international media coverage"
- Add "Identify logical fallacies in official statements"
