## Blog Post Template

**Description**: Create engaging, SEO-optimized blog posts with clear structure and value delivery.

```
Write a blog post about [TOPIC]:

**Target Audience**: [WHO WILL READ THIS]

**Reader's Goal**: [WHAT THEY WANT TO LEARN/ACHIEVE]

**Tone**: [PROFESSIONAL/CASUAL/TECHNICAL/CONVERSATIONAL]

**Key Points to Cover**:
- [POINT 1]
- [POINT 2]
- [POINT 3]

**SEO Requirements**:
- Primary keyword: [KEYWORD]
- Secondary keywords: [KEYWORD 1, KEYWORD 2]
- Target length: [WORD COUNT]
- Meta description: [YES/NO]

**Format Preferences**:
- Subheadings: [H2/H3 STRUCTURE]
- Include examples: [YES/NO]
- Include statistics/data: [YES/NO]
- Call-to-action: [WHAT ACTION YOU WANT]

Provide:
1. Attention-grabbing title (3 options)
2. Engaging introduction with hook
3. Well-structured body with subheadings
4. Actionable takeaways or conclusion
5. Meta description (150-160 characters)
6. Suggested internal/external links
```

**Usage Tips**: Include your brand voice guidelines or example posts for consistency. Specify expertise level to match your audience's knowledge base.

## Email Sequence Template

**Description**: Build multi-touch email campaigns that nurture leads and drive conversions.

```
Create an email sequence for [CAMPAIGN NAME]:

**Sequence Goal**: [ONBOARD/NURTURE/CONVERT/RE-ENGAGE]

**Audience**: [SUBSCRIBER TYPE AND STAGE]

**Sequence Structure**:
- Number of emails: [COUNT]
- Timing: [DAY 0, DAY 2, DAY 5, etc.]
- Trigger: [WHAT STARTS THE SEQUENCE]

**Business Context**:
- Product/service: [WHAT YOU'RE PROMOTING]
- Price point: [COST]
- Sales cycle: [SHORT/MEDIUM/LONG]
- Main objections: [BARRIERS TO PURCHASE]

**Content Strategy**:
- Email 1 focus: [WELCOME/EDUCATION/VALUE]
- Email 2 focus: [BUILD TRUST/ADDRESS OBJECTION]
- Email 3 focus: [SOCIAL PROOF/TESTIMONIALS]
- Email 4 focus: [SOFT SELL/DEMO]
- Email 5 focus: [DIRECT OFFER/URGENCY]

**Brand Voice**: [PERSONALITY AND TONE]

Provide for each email:
1. Subject line (2 variations)
2. Preview text
3. Email body copy
4. CTA copy and placement
5. Timing recommendation
6. Personalization tokens
7. Segment-specific variations
8. Exit criteria (when to stop sequence)
```

**Usage Tips**: Map sequence length to your sales cycle. Include behavioral triggers (opened but didn't click, clicked but didn't buy) for advanced automation.
