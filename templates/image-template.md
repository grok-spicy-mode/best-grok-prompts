## Product Photography Template

**Description**: Generate professional product images with studio-quality lighting and composition for e-commerce and marketing.

```
Generate a professional product photograph:

**Product Details**:
- Product: [ITEM NAME AND TYPE]
- Key features to highlight: [FEATURES TO EMPHASIZE]
- Brand aesthetic: [MINIMAL/LUXURY/PLAYFUL/TECHNICAL]
- Product dimensions: [SIZE CONTEXT]
- Material/texture: [METAL/FABRIC/GLASS/PLASTIC/etc.]

**Photography Style**:
- Shot type: [HERO SHOT/LIFESTYLE/DETAIL/FLAT LAY/360 VIEW]
- Angle: [EYE LEVEL/OVERHEAD/45 DEGREE/LOW ANGLE]
- Background: [WHITE/GRADIENT/LIFESTYLE SETTING/TRANSPARENT]
- Lighting: [SOFT/DRAMATIC/NATURAL/STUDIO/BACKLIT]

**Composition**:
- Product placement: [CENTER/RULE OF THIRDS/DIAGONAL]
- Props/context: [MINIMAL/LIFESTYLE PROPS/NONE]
- Color palette: [SPECIFIC COLORS OR MOOD]
- Depth of field: [SHALLOW/DEEP - BOKEH/SHARP]

**Technical Specifications**:
- Aspect ratio: [1:1/16:9/4:5/CUSTOM]
- Resolution needs: [SOCIAL/WEB/PRINT]
- Image mood: [CLEAN/WARM/COOL/VIBRANT/MUTED]

Example prompt:
"Professional product photography of [PRODUCT], centered on [BACKGROUND], studio lighting with soft shadows, shot from [ANGLE], shallow depth of field, [COLOR PALETTE] color grading, commercial photography style, high resolution, crisp focus on product details, minimalist composition"
```

**Usage Tips**: Be specific about lighting direction (front, side, rim lighting). Mention material properties for realistic texture rendering. For lifestyle shots, describe the context and user interaction.

## Portrait & Headshot Template

**Description**: Create professional or artistic portraits with consistent style, lighting, and character details.

```
Generate a portrait image:

**Subject Details**:
- Subject: [PERSON DESCRIPTION - age, gender, ethnicity if relevant]
- Expression: [SMILING/SERIOUS/CONFIDENT/THOUGHTFUL/NATURAL]
- Pose: [HEADSHOT/SHOULDERS UP/3/4 VIEW/PROFILE]
- Gaze direction: [CAMERA/OFF-CAMERA/DOWN/UP]
- Hair style: [DESCRIPTION]
- Clothing: [PROFESSIONAL/CASUAL/FORMAL/SPECIFIC STYLE]

**Photography Style**:
- Style: [CORPORATE HEADSHOT/EDITORIAL/ENVIRONMENTAL/FINE ART]
- Lighting setup: [REMBRANDT/BUTTERFLY/SPLIT/LOOP/BROAD/SHORT]
- Lighting quality: [SOFT/HARD/NATURAL WINDOW/STUDIO]
- Background: [SOLID COLOR/BLURRED/ENVIRONMENTAL/GRADIENT]

**Technical Settings**:
- Lens simulation: [50MM/85MM/35MM EQUIVALENT]
- Depth of field: [f/1.8 BOKEH/f/4 MODERATE/f/8 SHARP]
- Color treatment: [NATURAL/WARM/COOL/B&W/DESATURATED]
- Mood: [PROFESSIONAL/APPROACHABLE/ARTISTIC/DRAMATIC]

**Composition**:
- Framing: [TIGHT/MEDIUM/WIDE]
- Headroom: [MINIMAL/STANDARD/GENEROUS]

Example prompt:
"Professional corporate headshot, [SUBJECT DESCRIPTION], confident smile, direct eye contact, wearing [CLOTHING], soft Rembrandt lighting from camera left, neutral gray background slightly out of focus, shot with 85mm f/1.8 depth of field, warm color grading, shoulders and head visible, high-resolution portrait photography"
```

**Usage Tips**: For consistent character generation across multiple images, use very specific physical descriptions. Mention specific portrait lighting setups (Rembrandt, butterfly) for professional results.
